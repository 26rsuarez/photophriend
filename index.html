Weclome to PhotoPhriend!
Did you know that the term "photography" literally translates to "drawing with light?" It's true! So it only makes sense to know everything you can about the light conditions before you go out shooting. Here you will find up to date weather information, sunrise and sunset times to help plan your "golden hour" expedition, and a local map to help show you the surrounding area. Looking for some more bite sized tips? Click the random fact generator up top. Want some inspiration? Check out our photo gallery below. Every photo shown used only available light. No flash photography experience needed! Hopefully your time here at PhotoPhriend will prove en-"light"-ening. Happy shooting!
